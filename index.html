  • Text fades in word-by-word with 0.15s stagger
  • Eggplant-to-Ink gradient shifts slowly throughout
  • Subtle particle field drifts in background

  • Buttercup underline draws left-to-right beneath keyword
  • "MAYBE" pill badge pops in with bounce at 0.3s delay
  • SoFi nine-circle gem motif fades in lower-left corner
03 TRANSITION

Transition Beat

"Let's break it down."
  • Horizontal line draws center-outward with SoFi Blue gradient
  • Text types in with cursor blink effect
  • Three colored dots appear in sequence (0.2s stagger) previewing the color language ahead
  • Quick 2-second beat — pacing breath before content
04 DEFINITION
What is a cosigner?
DEFINITION A parent or trusted adult who shares legal responsibility for your loan.
0:10 – 0:18

Cosigner Definition

"A cosigner is someone, usually a parent or trusted adult, who agrees to share legal responsibility for your loan."
  • Two-person icon animates: primary figure (SoFi Blue) appears first, cosigner figure (Buttercup dashed outline) slides in alongside
  • Definition card slides up from bottom with stagger
  • "Legal responsibility" highlights in Buttercup with underline draw
  • Clean, educational tone — no alarm colors yet
05 STAKES

0:18 – 0:26

The Stakes — What's at Risk

"If you can't pay, they have to. It also shows up on their credit report. This is a big deal."
  • Two consequence cards slide in from left and right simultaneously
  • Warning triangle icon pulses in Cantaloupe
  • Credit report card shakes subtly on appear
  • Bottom warning bar slides up with Poppy accent — "This is a big deal" lands as emotional beat
  • Color shifts from educational blue to cautionary cantaloupe/poppy
06 EMPHASIS
Not a decision to take lightly.
0:26 – 0:30

Weight of the Decision

"…so it's not a decision either of you should take lightly."
  • Balance scale illustration with "YOU" and "COSIGNER" pans — both weighted equally
  • Scale balances level, then tilts slightly to emphasize shared responsibility
  • "Lightly" in Buttercup — gentle emphasis, not alarm
  • Pause beat — lets the stakes sink in before moving to positive framing

Why Most Students Need a Cosigner

"Most students need a cosigner on private loans because they don't have much credit history yet. That's completely normal."
  • Credit history meter animates to ~25% (low) in SoFi Blue
  • "LIMITED" marker appears with gentle pulse
  • "COMPLETELY NORMAL" badge pops in with reassuring bounce — shifts tone back to supportive
  • Color returns to SoFi Blue — safe, informational territory
08 BENEFIT

WITH COSIGNER 6.8%

0:38 – 0:44

The Upside — Better Interest Rates

"Adding a cosigner with strong credit can also help you qualify for better interest rates."
  • Two rate cards appear side by side — "Without" (Cantaloupe, high bar) vs "With" (SoFi Blue, low bar)
  • Arrow animates left-to-right connecting the two
  • Rate numbers count down from 12.5% to 6.8% with ticker animation
  • Positive reframe — cosigner isn't just a requirement, it's an advantage

Critical Question #1 — Financial Safety

"Before saying yes, both of you should ask: Can the cosigner absorb this debt if something goes wrong?"
  • "BEFORE SAYING YES, ASK:" eyebrow fades in first — sets up checklist mode
  • Large circled "1" slides in from left in SoFi Blue
  • "Absorb this debt" highlights in Cantaloupe — signals caution without alarm
  • "WORST CASE" and "PLAN B?" cards appear with 0.3s stagger
  • Tone is thoughtful, not scary — empowering the viewer to ask the right questions

2

Critical Question #2 — Cosigner Release

"And does the loan offer a cosigner release — meaning, can they eventually be removed from the loan once you've proven you can handle payments on your own?"
  • Circled "2" appears with same entrance as Q1 for visual consistency
  • Chain visual: two linked circles (YOU + COSIGNER) connected by dashed Buttercup line
  • Dashed line animates a "break" effect — chain separating symbolizes release
  • "Cosigner release?" in Buttercup — this is the key concept of the entire video

Typography

Headlines: Inter ExtraBold / Bold

Body: Inter Regular / Medium

Labels: Inter SemiBold, ALL-CAPS, tracked

Minimum size: 14px equivalent on 1080p

Animation Guidelines

Default easing: cubic-bezier(0.25, 0.1, 0.25, 1)

Stagger delay: 0.2–0.3s between elements

Text reveals: fade + slight translateY(8px)

Icon animations: scale from 0.8 to 1.0

Respect reduced-motion preferences
